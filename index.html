Coding Boot Camps

Coding Boot Camps are sprouting up all over America. Universities and high schools have bolstered their curriculums by offering students the opportunity to learn all about html, css, js and other applications in a fast-paced environment. Even adults are furthering their education by enrolling in these courses.The job market in the U.S. is projected a rise of 26% in the web development field within the next three years. If students can keep up with the pace of the classes, they have an excellent chance of landing a very rewarding and long-term position.

Classes

Classes are usually scheduled three to four times a week, with each class lasting between three and four hours. Although the classes are integral for learning coding applications, it is the work away from work as well as the time the student puts into his craft that will deem if he is successful.

Tuition

The tuition for Coding Boot Camps can be exorbitant. The prices can range from $6,500 to $10,000 for a six-month course. But the investment may seem small when considering a career that does not require the cost of a four-year degree.
